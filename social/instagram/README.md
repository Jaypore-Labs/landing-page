# Instagram rebrand + launch kit — Jaypore Labs

If I were running social for a friendly product studio, here's how I'd open the Jaypore Labs IG account. The strategy and every asset is ready to upload.

---

## Why Instagram at all (for a B2B studio)

Operators and founders still browse it. They don't post much there but they remember what they saw. Our goal on IG is **brand memory + discoverability**, not direct lead gen. A consistent, editorial feed means the next time someone hears "Jaypore Labs" from a peer they recognise the visual DNA.

We play to our strengths: typography, editorial voice, unusual juxtapositions (see `.claude/skills/daily-article.md` — the "elephant on a bicycle" content philosophy). We do **not** compete with lifestyle or meme accounts.

## 3. The opening grid (first 9 feed posts)

The "grid" is what a visitor sees when they land on your profile. The first 9 posts define the brand. This grid is designed with visual rhythm: bold positioning → manifesto → case → playbook → closing CTA.

Post in **reverse order** (last upload = top-left of the grid). So upload `09-industry-first.png` first, then `08-first-reply.png`, …, ending with `01-welcome.png` which lands top-left.

Or post sequentially over 9 days (recommended pace for a new account) at 10am IST so they build momentum without looking spammy.

## 4. Story highlights (5 covers — circle-cropped on profile)

Highlights are the pinned reels above the feed. They let visitors jump to categories of your content without scrolling. Five is the sweet spot for a B2B account.

| Cover | Highlight label | What goes inside |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `highlights/work.png` | **WORK** | Selected work highlights — screens, stats, and short write-ups. |
| `highlights/process.png` | **PROCESS** | How-we-work stories: Friday demo rituals, eval frameworks, discovery week outputs. |
| `highlights/craft.png` | **CRAFT** | Design / engineering details we're proud of — a typography choice, an eval harness, a migration note. |
| `highlights/hello.png` | **HELLO** | Intro story, engagement models, how to reach us. |
| `highlights/journal.png` | **JOURNAL** | Teasers of daily blog articles (see `.claude/skills/daily-article.md`). |

### How to set them up

1. Create a dummy archived story (upload the highlight cover PNG as a story, add it to archive).
2. Tap **Story Highlight** → **New** → select the story → tap **Edit Cover** → upload the matching PNG.
3. Name the highlight.
4. Add more stories to the highlight over time.

## 5. First-month content cadence

| Week | Mon | Wed | Fri |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | `01-welcome` | `03-track-record` | `05-playbook` (carousel) |
| 2 | `02-manifesto` | `04-capabilities` | `06-no-buzzword` |
| 3 | `07-ship-weekly` | `08-first-reply` | `09-industry-first` |
| 4 | Daily-article carousel #1 | carousel #2 | Field-note single post |

Starting week 4, switch to **one daily-article carousel per week** (use `/daily-article` — see `.claude/skills/daily-article.md`) plus two field notes.

**Stories:** aim for 3-5 stories per week. Mix: behind-scenes ("this week we shipped…"), quotes, polls ("which is the bigger AI adoption blocker: compliance or culture?"), build-log screenshots.

**Reels:** don't force them early. Once the blog cadence is steady, turn one carousel per week into a 15-second Reel (screen-record the slides with a voice-over).

## 6. Regenerate any asset

All commands below run at retina 2× by default.

```bash
# Opening-grid post (example: the welcome)
npm run gen:post -- --template quote --size square \
  --title "Your business,\nAI-ready." \
  --subtitle "We build AI-enabled software and help businesses put AI to work." \
  --kicker "Jaypore Labs · 01" \
  --out social/instagram/grid/01-welcome.png

# Story highlight cover
npm run gen:post -- --template logo --size avatar \
  --title "WORK" --subtitle "" \
  --out social/instagram/highlights/work.png

# Daily-article carousel (see the daily-article skill for full flow)
npm run gen:post -- --template quote --size portrait \
  --title "..." --subtitle "..." --kicker "Journal · 2026-04-20" \
  --out social/carousels/<slug>/slide-01.png
```

See `.claude/skills/post-image.md` for every template + flag.
